Useful Numbers
Keep these handy while traveling in the United States.
  • 911 — Emergency (police, fire, ambulance)
  • 511 — Traffic & road conditions (many states)
  • 988 — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  • 1-800-222-1222 — Poison Control
  • 311 — Non-emergency city services (where available)
Dial 911 only for real emergencies. Save your country’s embassy number too.
